Join the Group

If you are an undergraduate at Washington University interested in research with our group, please send a short email about your interests and background, with resume/CV and any previous research papers, to chongjie at wustl.edu.

If you are interested in joining our group as a Ph.D student, follow the procedure on the admission page (https://engineering.wustl.edu/academics/graduate-admissions/index.html).

Postdoctoral research positions are available in reinforcement learning and robotics. If you are interested and will hold a PhD degree (or be close to completion) in Computer Science or related discipline together with relevant experience and also have a strong publication record in machine learning or closely-related area, please send an email with your resume/CV, research statement, and selected research papers, to chongjie at wustl.edu.
