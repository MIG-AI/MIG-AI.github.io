PhD Candidate
Mengminwei Science and Technology Building, Tsinghua University, Haidian, Beijing, 100084, China
cheng-h18@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn

I am a PhD student in the Machine Learning Group(MIG) at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University, starting from September 2018. My research interests include artificial intelligence(AI) and reinforcement learning. I mainly committed to the study of model-based RL and robotics. Model-based RL is an important area in reinforcement learning, which has a wide range of application in robots and other modern devices. At the same time, how to efficiently combine model-free RL and model-based RL is also an important area, which makes an important role in alleviating the sample efficiency of RL.
